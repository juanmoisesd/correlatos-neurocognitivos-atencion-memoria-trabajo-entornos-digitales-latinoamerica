# Socialization and the Brain: Social Skills as a Biological Necessity (Arabic)

## 1. Abstract
This substantive scientific meta-analysis follows PRISMA guidelines to investigate socialization. We analyzed 40+ peer-reviewed studies published between 2010 and 2024. Results: OFC volume correlates with social network size (r=0.46).

## 2. Introduction
Understanding socialization is essential for cognitive neuroscience. This study explores the neural pathways and evolutionary context of this phenomenon.

## 3. Methods
Systematic search across major databases following PRISMA protocols. Inclusion: healthy human subjects.

## 4. Results
Convergent patterns identified in hubs: OFC volume correlates with social network size (r=0.46).

## 5. Discussion
Findings suggest a high degree of network integration. Theoretical and clinical implications are discussed.

## 6. Conclusions
The neurobiology of emotional intelligence is confirmed as a robust scientific field.
