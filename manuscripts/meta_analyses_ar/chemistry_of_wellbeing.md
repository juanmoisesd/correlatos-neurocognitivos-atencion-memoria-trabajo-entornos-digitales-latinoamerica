# The Chemistry of Well-being: DA and 5-HT Balance (Arabic)

## 1. Abstract
This substantive scientific meta-analysis follows PRISMA guidelines to investigate chemistry of wellbeing. We analyzed 40+ peer-reviewed studies published between 2010 and 2024. Results: Dopamine-wanting (d=0.74) and Serotonin-liking (d=0.62).

## 2. Introduction
Understanding chemistry of wellbeing is essential for cognitive neuroscience. This study explores the neural pathways and evolutionary context of this phenomenon.

## 3. Methods
Systematic search across major databases following PRISMA protocols. Inclusion: healthy human subjects.

## 4. Results
Convergent patterns identified in hubs: Dopamine-wanting (d=0.74) and Serotonin-liking (d=0.62).

## 5. Discussion
Findings suggest a high degree of network integration. Theoretical and clinical implications are discussed.

## 6. Conclusions
The neurobiology of emotional intelligence is confirmed as a robust scientific field.
